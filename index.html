God and Goddess Character Cards

Zeus

King of the Gods

Zeus is the ruler of the gods and the sky. He is the god of lightning and thunder, and is known for his mighty strength and wrath. He is usually portrayed as an older man with a white beard and a lightning bolt in his hand.

  • Attributes:
  • Strength
  • Power
  • Wisdom
  • Symbols:
  • Lightning Bolt
  • Eagle
  • Oak Tree

Hera

Hera is the goddess of marriage and women. She is the wife of Zeus and is known for her jealous and vengeful nature. She is usually portrayed as a beautiful woman with a crown and a peacock.

  • Attributes:
  • Loyalty
  • Fidelity
  • Protection
  • Symbols:
  • Peacock
  • Pomegranate
  • Cow

Ares

God of War

Ares is the god of war and violence. He is known for his strength and courage in battle, but also for his lack of intelligence and wisdom. He is usually portrayed as a young man carrying a shield and a spear.

  • Attributes:
  • Courage
  • Strength
  • Ferocity
  • Symbols:
  • Spear
  • Shield
  • Dog

Aphrodite

Goddess of Love and Beauty

Aphrodite is the goddess of love and beauty. She is known for her beauty and charm, and is often depicted as a beautiful woman with a dove or swan. She is also the goddess of passion, desire, and fertility.

  • Attributes:
  • Beauty
  • Charm
  • Sensuality
  • Symbols:
  • Dove
  • Swan
  • Rose

Poseidon

God of the Sea

Poseidon is the god of the sea and all water. He is known for his temperamental nature and is often depicted as a mature man with a trident in his hand. He is associated with earthquakes, storms, and horses.

  • Attributes:
  • Power
  • Strength
  • Fury
  • Symbols:
  • Trident
  • Horse
  • Octopus
